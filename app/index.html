- WHAT IS LOÓNA? loóna is the first app that lets you quickly disconnect from a long stressful day and get in the right mood for sleep. - SO, IT'S ANOTHER SLEEP APP, RIGHT? Not exactly. loóna isn't a list of direct go-to-sleep techniques, but rather a mood-altering app that prepares you emotionally for going to sleep. - WHY IS BEDTIME MOOD IMPORTANT? Negative emotions we accumulate during the day are processed and consolidated by our brain during sleep making them more difficult to disassociate from when faced again in the future. Moreover, feeling angry, anxious, down, or, the opposite, excited, and elated, is likely to affect latencies to sleep onset and REM-sleep. People mistake it for the symptoms of a sleep disorder, but in reality, they just may be in the wrong mood for sleep. - HOW DOES LOÓNA WORK? Each night you'll get a new sleepscape. A sleepscape is a guided session that combines activity-based relaxation, storytelling, and sounds in a unique way. Complete it to shut the frantic world out, reset your mind, and create the perfect mood for sleep. NB! The app is currently available only in English. Need more daytime activities, otherwise great
Blu_Jess
As the title says, this app is excellent and I would recommend it to anybody, however, it is sorely lacking in daytime activities. The sleep scapes are absolutely wonderful and so relaxing, but I’d love to have maybe “dayscapes” to do when I’m anxious or stressed. Normally I wouldn’t complain because technically I wasn’t promised anything like this, but given that all of the advertisements for this app show tons of things to color, one would get the impression you can color scapes at will if you feel stressed or need a break. Not so, you can only color one sleep scape per night. If you wanted to recolor it you could start it again the next day but again it would be much better if they had something to color during the day. They definitely have sounds and relaxing things to use but no coloring activities except one per night. What I did really enjoy about the nightscape was the relaxing narration and fun facts about objects I was coloring. All in all I think it’s a great app but I would love to see more things to color during the day, and also more choices rather than just the scape of the night or something. more
